Wind energy is a renewable energy source that converts the kinetic energy of wind into electrical or mechanical power. It is generated using wind turbines, which capture the movement of air and transform it into a clean, sustainable energy source. Wind energy is poised to play a significant role in meeting global energy demands while reducing environmental impact.
